Wine

Wine is an alcoholic drink typically made from fermented grapes. It has been produced for thousands of years, with the earliest evidence of wine is from the Caucasus region in today's Georgia (6000 BCE), Persia (5000 BCE), and Italy (4000 BCE).

Wine Classification

There are many different types of wines, each with their own characteristics. To find out more about how wines are classified, click on "Wine Classification".

Wine Making Process

Although wine was first produced about 8000 years ago, there has been little changes to the wine making process. To find out more about the wine making process, as well as the differences between red winemaking and white winemaking, click on "Wine Making Process".

Uses of Wine

Besides for drinking, there are many different ways wine can be used. The microbiology, alcohol, and chemical makeup enable a variety of applications in places such as the home and the kitchen. To find out more, click on "Uses of Wine".
